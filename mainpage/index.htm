Both my parents are of the Batak ethnic group, my mom hails from Karo and my dad from Toba.

This is the unofficial Batak flag.


White(bontar in Toba Batak) represents the heavens, the upper world, or Banua Ginjang (lit. the High World). It is the abode of the gods, with the head of them being Debata Mula Jadi na Bolon (lit. the great god of the beginning), who is the agreed upon Batak High God, still worshipped by the Parmalim, modern day animists in North Sumatra.

Red(rara in Toba Batak) represents our world, the middle world, or Banua Tonga (lit. Middle World). It was all water before Sideak Parujar, the granddaughter of the High God, descended upon it and subsequently paralyzed Naga Padoha (Padoha dragon), before sprinkling soil on top of it, creating the Earth.

Black (birong in Toba Batak) represents the under world, the residency of Naga/Raja Padoha (lit. Padoha king/dragon). It is akin to the afterlife of the ancient animistic religion.
